# Changelog

## [1.0.0] — 2026-07-12
- Initial public release: 70 Cowork-native prompts across 7 categories (documents & reports, spreadsheets & data, presentations, research & summarization, email & communication, project management, team workflows & knowledge).
- `anatomy.md` — the 5-part frame (Inputs, Task, Output, Standards, Guardrails) every prompt follows.
- ~35 prompts are single-run versions of the [25 team workflows](https://github.com/claude-cowork-training/claude-cowork-team-workflows), each linking to its parent workflow.
